HALF-KELLY POSITION SIZING:
  Formula:
    Kelly% = (Win_Rate * Avg_Win - Loss_Rate * Avg_Loss) / Avg_Win
    Half_Kelly% = Kelly% / 2
    Position_Size = (Account_Balance * Half_Kelly%) / Max_Risk_Per_Contract

  Max Risk Per Contract:
    - GEX PIN spreads: $250 ($5 wide - $2.50 credit)
    - OTM Single-Sided: $900 ($10 wide - $1.00 credit, conservative)

  Bootstrap Phase:
    - First 10 trades: 1 contract (build statistics)
    - After 10 trades: Rolling 50-trade statistics for Kelly calculation
    - Safety halt: Stop trading if account drops below 50% of starting capital

====================================================================================================================================================================================
REAL-WORLD IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
====================================================================================================================================================================================

FOR LIVE TRADING:

1. **Starting Capital**: $20,000 recommended minimum
   - Too small (<$10k): Position sizing becomes ineffective
   - Too large (>$50k): Can increase MAX_CONTRACTS to 5-10

2. **Bootstrap Phase**: Critical first 10 trades
   - All at 1 contract to build statistics
   - Don't skip this phase (tempting to start with more)
   - Let the Kelly formula learn your actual win rate

3. **Rolling Statistics**: 50-trade window
   - Balances responsiveness vs stability
   - Too short (<20): Noisy, erratic sizing
   - Too long (>100): Slow to adapt to regime changes

4. **Safety Halt**: 50% threshold
   - If account drops to $10,000, stop trading
   - Re-evaluate strategy before resuming
   - Don't disable this protection!

5. **Max Risk Calculation**: Must be accurate
   - GEX: $5 spread width - entry credit = max risk
   - OTM: $10 spread width - entry credit = max risk
   - Use conservative estimates if unsure

6. **Contract Cap**: Start conservative
   - 3 contracts for $20k account (current simulation)
   - 5 contracts for $50k account
   - 10 contracts for $100k+ account
   - Scale gradually as account grows
